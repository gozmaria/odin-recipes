Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup, made of a fish stock called “dashi” and softened miso paste. This simple soup is filling and satisfying due to its high protein content and versatile to customize it to everyone's taste and what you have on hand. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dashi stock
  • 2-3 tablespoons red or white miso paste
  • 4 oz silken tofu
  • 2 green onions

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, bring the stock to a boil.
  2. Once stock is boiled, turn the heat down to low. Transfer about 1 cup of stock to a cup and stir in miso paste. Stir until miso paste is fully dissolved.
  3. Pour the miso mixture back into the stock.
  4. Add tofu and green onion. Simmer gently for 2 minutes and serve. (TIP: Don't bring the soup to a boil after adding miso paste!)
